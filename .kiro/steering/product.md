# Product Overview

This is a **Repair Shop Management App** built for mobile and web platforms. The application provides comprehensive job tracking, customer management, and billing functionality for repair shop operations.

## Core Features

- **Job Management**: Track repair jobs from creation to completion with status updates, priority levels, and technician assignments
- **Customer Portal**: Allow customers to view their repair jobs, track progress, and receive notifications
- **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive management interface for shop owners and managers
- **Multi-Role Support**: Different interfaces for customers, technicians, admins, and owners
- **Real-time Updates**: Job status tracking and notifications system
- **Billing Integration**: Cost estimation, parts tracking, and payment management

## Target Users

- **Customers**: Submit and track repair requests
- **Technicians**: Manage assigned jobs and update progress
- **Admins/Managers**: Oversee operations, manage staff, and generate reports
- **Shop Owners**: Access comprehensive analytics and business insights

## Business Domain

The app handles device repair workflows including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other electronic devices. It manages the complete lifecycle from initial customer request through diagnosis, repair, parts ordering, completion, and delivery.
